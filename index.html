Can Deep Cleaning Improve Indoor Air Quality in Ottawa Homes?

Yes, deep cleaning can significantly improve indoor air quality by removing hidden dust, allergens, bacteria, and pollutants that regular cleaning cannot eliminate. Hiring a deep cleaning service Ottawa helps eliminate contaminants from carpets, furniture, and hard-to-reach areas, creating a cleaner, healthier living environment.

Indoor air pollution can be 2–5x worse than outdoor air due to trapped dust, allergens, and bacteria.

Why Indoor Air Quality Is a Serious Problem

In Ottawa, homes remain closed for long winter months, which traps indoor pollutants. Over time, these pollutants accumulate and circulate in your living space.

  • Dust particles from daily activities
  • Pet dander and fur
  • Pollen entering from outside
  • Mold from moisture-prone areas
  • Bacteria on surfaces and fabrics
